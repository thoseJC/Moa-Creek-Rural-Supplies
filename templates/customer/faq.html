Frequently Asked Questions

Moa Creek Rural Supplies is an online store that sells and delivers a wide range of rural agricultural products across New Zealand. We specialize in categories such as animal health care, dairy hygiene, fencing, machinery, and more.
We are based in Alexandra, Central Otago, New Zealand.
You can place an order by browsing our products, adding items to your shopping cart, and proceeding to checkout. You will need to select your shipping options and make a payment to complete your order.
We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards and gift cards. Detailed payment options are available at checkout.
